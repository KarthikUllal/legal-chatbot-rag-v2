About This Project

This AI-based legal chatbot is designed to make Indian laws accessible to everyone. Using advanced Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) technology, it provides accurate, verified legal information in simple language.

Whether you're a student, researcher, or someone seeking legal guidance, this chatbot helps you understand your rights and responsibilities under Indian law.
